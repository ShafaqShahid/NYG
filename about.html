About US

Youth Empowerment Society is a non-political & non- sectarian organization that works on capacity building and promotes patriotic culture by connecting & transferring the treasured heritage of our cultural icons & national heroes for Reviving Unity & Raising an Intellectual Youth for Greater Pakistan.

The Institute of Sports (IPS) is a transformative organization leveraging the power of sports for positive change. It provides a platform for youth to showcase their talents and contribute to the nation's prosperity. Through meticulous planning, IPS organizes events, manages academies, engages in advocacy, and cultivates corporate sponsorships. It offers free support to aspiring athletes, conducts talent hunts, and fosters a culture of excellence. IPS aims to promote a vibrant sports culture, instill fundamental values, and ensure equal opportunities for all aspiring athletes.

The National Youth Games Pakistan is a groundbreaking initiative aimed at revolutionizing the landscape of youth athletics in the country. With a unique approach, these games promise to establish an enduring presence in society by providing an annual showcase of youthful talent. The structure of the games is meticulously planned, with a grand mega-event held every four years, focusing on different levels of competition: School Games, University Games, Provincial Youth Games, and culminating in the National Youth Games for athletes up to 16 years old.
