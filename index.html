About NYG


The National Youth Games Pakistan is a groundbreaking initiative aimed at revolutionizing the landscape of youth athletics in the country. With a unique approach, these games promise to establish an enduring presence in society by providing an annual showcase of youthful talent. The structure of the games is meticulously planned, with a grand mega-event held every four years, focusing on different levels of competition: School Games, University Games, Provincial Youth Games, and culminating in the National Youth Games for athletes up to 16 years old.

Our Mission


Our mission is empowering the youth through the pursuit of sports excellence and contribute to national building. We are dedicated to nurturing raw talent, fostering inclusivity, and equipping young athletes with the tools they need to thrive on the global stage.


Our Vision


Our vision is to cultivate a generation of empowered youth, equipped with the skills and mindset to excel in sports, thereby contributing to national pride and unity. Through the National Youth Games, we aim to inspire dreams of international success while fostering a culture of excellence and solidarity within our nation.
