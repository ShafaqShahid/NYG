Executive Summary

Instituition Brief

The Institute for Promotion of Sports (IPS) is dedicated to leveraging sports as a catalyst for national development and fostering responsible citizens. Through strategic partnerships, meticulous planning, and advocacy efforts, IPS aims to promote a vibrant sports culture and instill essential values in the youth, thereby contributing to societal transformation and prosperity.

ProjectBrief

The National Youth Games Pakistan (NYG) is a revolutionary movement aimed at transforming youth athletics across the nation. With a focus on talent scouting, skill development, and competitive exposure, NYG provides a clear pathway for young athletes to excel both nationally and internationally. Through financial support and strategic planning, NYG empowers athletes from all backgrounds, fostering a dynamic society of sporting excellence.

Executional Strategy

NYG's executional strategy involves collaboration with key stakeholders, including the National Olympic Committee, government, sports federations, and corporate sponsors. By ensuring regulatory adherence, securing funding, and providing athlete support, NYG paves the way for the successful execution of its multi-tiered games, ultimately aiming for global recognition and Olympic participation.

Potential Impact

The potential impact of NYG extends beyond sports, fostering societal transformation, holistic well-being, personal and economic development, and national pride. By inspiring young athletes, promoting a healthy lifestyle, and contributing to economic growth, NYG aims to elevate Pakistan's sports reputation and strengthen its global presence.

Our Unique Approach

NYG's unique approach involves innovative partnerships, holistic mentorship, cultural and environmental integration, inclusivity, and aspirations. By bridging gaps between stakeholders and emphasizing ethical development, NYG creates a platform for young athletes to pursue their Olympic dreams while fostering camaraderie and excellence.

Project Background

Driven by the recognition of Pakistan's untapped athletic potential and global trends in sports development, NYG aims to address challenges such as the absence of a structured sports system and inadequate talent nurturing schemes. By creating a comprehensive sports system, NYG seeks to elevate Pakistan's sports status and foster a strong sports culture.

Council of Patrons

NYG's leadership structure comprises a Council of Patrons and Executives, including esteemed individuals from various sectors, to ensure the success of the initiative. With a Patron-in-Chief leading the Council of Patrons, a Chairman overseeing the Steering Committee, a Secretary managing the Organizing Committee, and Brand Ambassadors promoting the project's mission, NYG's leadership facilitates strategic guidance, operational efficiency, and collaboration among stakeholders, thereby advancing the project's mission and vision.
